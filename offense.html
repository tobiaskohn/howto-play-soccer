Offense

Welcome to the offense page. Here, I will show you the best tricks and tips to be the best offensive player in your team.

1. Be fit

This might seem like a no-brainer, but playing an offensive position, such as winger, or striker, will require you to chase after passes, and dribble quickly past defenders more often than you think. You can practice this by going out for jogs, but doing sprinting exerices will also be nesessary if you want to be in good shape for soccer.

2. Have a good touch

Having a good touch is crucial in order to be a good player. Out in the pitch, you will need to make accurate passes, accurately cross in the ball, and recieve passes with your foot. One of the best exercises to getting a good touch is juggling. This will require you to kick the ball at the right strength, and then flicking it back up.

3. Be good with both feet

Most soccer players are right-footed, and most of them struggle kicking with their left. However, being good with your strong and your weak foot will give you many advantages over your defenders. Defenders usually like to force you to your weak foot, so if you are good with both, you will be unstoppable. Some ways to practice your weak foot is kicking the ball against the wall, only with your bad foot. or shooting a ball at a goal with it.
