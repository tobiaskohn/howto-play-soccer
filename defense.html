Defense

Welcome to the defending page. In a soccer team, the defense is probably the most inportant part of the team. They do everything. They stop other attakers from scoring goals, and they contribute to the offense by scoring goals.

1. Don't be scared to hurt you oponent

When you go in for a tackle, go in as hard as you can. Don't think about hurting your oponent, because if you do, your tackle will probably be unsuccessful, and you oponent will just keep bribbling towards your goal.

2. Get low, sideways, and on your toes

When you're going 1 vs 1 with an attacker, you want to get low, sideways, and on your toes. Get sideways in a way where you force your oponent to the sideline, or wherever you want him to go.

3. Give directions

As the furthest line back in your team, you will have a vision of the whole field, therefore you will be expected to give your teammates directions. Tell them who they need to mark, if the team nneds to shift, or if you want them to step up.
